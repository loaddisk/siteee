## absence's shape ##
22/04/2026

The letting go of what isn’t my own is a very freeing feeling. The rope that I have held a strong grip on as it pulls against my being, has created a burn - one that will heal with time. As it is released, I notice a divot where it once lay. Rather than placing the rope where I once believed it ought to have belonged; to complete and reunite with the divot - I will allow it to heal on its own. 

I never needed the rope to complete the anatomy of my functioning hand, which is now free for atoms to flow through as they please.
